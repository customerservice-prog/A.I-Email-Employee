Friendly Party Rental — FAQ (official placeholder for local dev)

Where do you deliver?
We routinely serve the metro core and inner suburbs. Longer hauls are scheduled case-by-case; mileage fees may apply.

How early should I book?
Prime spring and fall weekends often sell out 6–10 weeks ahead. If your date is firm, a deposit is the best way to secure tents and seating.

What happens if it rains?
Frame tents can be fitted with sidewalls. We recommend deciding on sidewalls when booking if the forecast is uncertain—same-day adds may be limited during peak season.

What is your cancellation policy?
• 14+ days before the event: refund of deposit minus a $75 admin fee.
• 7–13 days before: 50% of the rental total is non-refundable.
• Within 7 days: contract total is due in full.

Do you clean equipment between events?
Chairs and tables are inspected and sanitized between rentals. Heavy food service may incur additional cleaning if items return excessively soiled.

Can you work with purchase orders?
Yes for qualified organizations. Reference PO numbers on the signed quote; net terms require credit approval.

How do I request a formal quote?
Reply with event date, venue address, approximate guest count, and any must-have items (tent size, chair style, linens). We return a line-item quote you can forward to finance.

What if I need changes after booking?
Contact us as soon as plans shift. We will adjust the contract when inventory allows; rush changes near the event date may have fees.
